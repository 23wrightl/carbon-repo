Introduction

Carbon Footprint

[kahr-buhn foot-print]

Carbon footprint is the amount of greenhouse gases that are released by one's actions. Greenhouse gases found in Earth's atmosphere trap heat which leads to increased planet heating also known as climate change or the greenhouse effect.

Donation Hubs

Team Trees

#teamtrees is a world-wide organization that plants one tree for every dollar donated! Having planted 9 million trees already, they are working toward 20 million!

Team Seas

This competition-style organization cleans the plastic and waste from oceans. Mr Beast participates in beach cleanups, and Mark Rober uses The Ocean Cleanup's technology to pick up trash from flowing waters.

National Forest Foundation

They planted 5.6 million trees in 2020, working toward conserving our forests. Chartered by Congress, their mission is to "bring people together to restore and enhance our National Forests and Grasslands."
