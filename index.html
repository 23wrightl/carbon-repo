Introduction

Carbon Footprint

[kahr-buhn foot-print]

Your carbon footprint is the amount of greenhouse gases that are released by your own actions. Greenhouse gases are gases found in Earth's atmosphere that trap heat which then leads to increased planet heating and the greenhouse effect or climate change.

External Resources and Donation Hubs

Team Trees

#teamtrees is a world-wide organization that plants one tree for every dollar donated! They have planeted 9 million trees already and are working toward 20 million trees!

Team Seas

In a competition between Mark Rober and Mr Beast, this organization works to clean the plastic and waste from oceans. Mr Beast participates in beach cleanups, and Mark Rober works to use a system of nets to grab trash from flowing waters using The Ocean Cleaup's Technology.

National Forest Foundation

Having planted 5.6 million trees in 2020, this foundation works toward conserving our forests. Chartered by Congress, their mission is to "bring people together to restore and enhance our National Forests and Grasslands."
